About Student Form Management System

The Student Form Management System (Stud Form) is a modern web-based platform designed to simplify the process of handling student-related forms and academic records. It provides a centralized solution for submitting, tracking, and managing various types of student data, from applications to updates and requests.

This system is built to serve both students and educational administrators by eliminating paperwork, streamlining communication, and improving data accuracy.
